⚙️ Word Engineers

Build words using three roots: -GRAPH- (write) · -SCOPE- (look) · -PORT- (carry)

📋

How to Build a Word

  1. Click a tile on the LEFT or RIGHT panel to pick it up
  2. Then click the drop zone in the table to place it — or just drag & drop!
  3. When both PREFIX and MEANING are placed, the Word column lights up ✨
  4. Use the 🔨 Build Your Own section below to freely combine any prefix + root!
  5. Click any placed tile to remove it and try again
-GRAPH-= write / describe
-SCOPE-= look / examine
-PORT-= carry
